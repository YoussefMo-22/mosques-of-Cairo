The History and The Features Of This Era

In 1525 the Ottoman administration of Egypt was defined and codified by the Ottoman grand vizier , Ibrahim Pasha , who was dispatched to Egypt for this purpose by the sultan Suleyman I.
In 1805A.D the Albanian commander Muhammed Ali sezied power and massacred the Mamluks with temporary approval of the Ottomans.
He ruled an effectively independent Sulatnate and institued many military , economic ,and cultural reforms .
He considered the founder of modern Egypt.
